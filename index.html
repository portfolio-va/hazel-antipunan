FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know before we start working together.

What services do you offer?+
I offer four main services: Social Media Management (content, design, scheduling, community management), Executive VA & Admin Support (calendar, inbox, documents, SOPs), Website & Funnel Development (WordPress and Canva-based), and Logistics & Operations coordination. I can also combine these depending on what your business needs most.
How do we get started?+
Book a free 30-minute discovery call. We talk about your business, your biggest pain points, and which service would make the most immediate difference. I then send a clear proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing. Once signed, you get an onboarding kit and we begin. The whole process from first call to first deliverable is typically under a week.
What are your rates?+
SMM packages start at ₱55,000/month for 2 platforms. Website packages start at ₱15,000 one-time. Funnel packages start at ₱10,000 one-time. For Executive VA and Admin support, rates are discussed on the discovery call based on scope and hours. I also offer custom bundles if you need more than one service.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes, absolutely. Most of my SMM clients are businesses in Canada and the United States. I am comfortable working across time zones and have experience managing US, Canadian, and Philippine clients simultaneously. All communication is remote and I adapt to your preferred timezone.
What timezone are you in?+
I am in Philippine Standard Time (PST / UTC+8). However, I regularly work adjusted hours to overlap with US Eastern, Central, and Mountain time for client communication. For ongoing retainer clients, we agree on overlap hours during onboarding.
Do you offer one-time projects or only retainers?+
Both. Website creation and sales funnels are one-time projects with a clear deliverable and support period. Social media management and executive VA work are monthly retainers. Some admin and SOP projects are also one-time. We discuss the best structure on the discovery call.
What tools do you use?+
Google Workspace (Sheets, Docs, Drive, Gmail), Google App Script, WordPress, Canva, Photoshop, Buffer, Metricool, Meta Business Suite, Trello, Notion, WhatsApp Business, Zoom, DingTalk, Google Analytics, and Calendly. I adapt to your existing tools as well.
Can you handle urgent or rush projects?+
Yes, for the right projects. If you have a time-sensitive need, message me directly and we will discuss scope and availability. Rush timelines are accommodated when my schedule allows, and I will always be upfront with you if I cannot commit to the timeline needed.
What makes you different from other VAs?+
Five years of real operations experience at a national logistics company, combined with hands-on expertise across four distinct service areas. I do not just complete tasks — I build systems, document processes, and communicate proactively. I treat your business like my own, which means I notice problems and fix them before you ask.
